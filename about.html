Our Story

Willow & Glow was founded by a Melbourne mum with a simple belief: beautiful fragrances should elevate everyday living. We create luxurious, statement reed diffusers that are as visually elegant as they are beautifully scented. Every diffuser is thoughtfully designed to become part of your home's décor while creating an atmosphere of comfort, warmth, and sophistication.
